Enchiladas

A great ground beef enchiladas recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 6 tortillas
  • 1 can of red enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup of cheese

directions

  1. Cook ground beef on stovetop until brown
  2. Preheat Oven to 350 degress F
  3. Warm tortillas in the microwave for 5-10 seconds
  4. Wrap ground beef evenly in all tortillas
  5. OPTIONAL: put cheese in tortillas with ground beef
  6. Place all tortillas in baking pan and pour red sause on them
  7. Spread your cup of cheese on the enchiladas evenly
  8. Bake for 15 minutes or until done
