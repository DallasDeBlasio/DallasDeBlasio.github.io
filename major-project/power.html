Adding Power

Adding power to your serve is something that everyone wants to do. It takes time to develop a powerful serve. What you need to keep in mind while serving is to be sure you are balanced and loaded in trophy position. This lets you explode upwards towards the ball and get as much rotational energy transfered to the ball as possible. Additiaonlly, at this stage you can start adding the step forward for the follow through to conserve as much forward momentum.

here shows how you can land in a way to get as much energy as possible into your swing

Tips

Don't expect to get every serve in, especially when adding power to it. When you are are trying to add power, try to miss long so you get the feel for hitting the ball harder. Then tone it back a little and add some more spin until you find the sweet spot. It takes time and repetition to get it where you what it to be, so be patient.

Common Mistakes

A lot of people try to wind up their swing too much, and it ends up making them off balance or making their swinging motion very segmented. Try to only wind up only as much as feels natural.
