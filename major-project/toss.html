Tossing the Ball

When holding the ball, you want it to rest in the groove where your fingers meet your hand in your non-dominate hand. then, when you toss the ball, you want to use only your arm to throw it in the air slightly forward and towards your dominate hand. Additionally, you want to be standing with your foot almost to the baseline and facing your dominate side instead of facing the net.

This shows how to hold the tennis ball

This should be the position you reach when tossing the ball

Tips

if you are facing forward towards the net, what you can do is place your racket down flat on the ground next to you with the head of the racket facing towards the net. it should also be just slightly further than shoulder width. now turn and get in your tossing stance. When you toss the ball, it should land on the racket strings. Thats now you know your toss is going where you want it to.

Common Mistakes

A common mistake is when people toss the ball using their whole body. This is not a good idea as you lose power for your actual swing if you do this. When you toss, you only want to bend your knees when you release the ball so that you are loaded for the swinging motion.
