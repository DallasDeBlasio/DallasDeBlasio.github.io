Types of Serves

You have now learned how to do a flat serve! There is a little bit more to learn, but you now have all the basics on how to hit a topspin ball off the serve. There are 2 other common types of serves. They are a slice serve and a kick serve. Each serve requires a slightly different setup. For a slice serve, you want to toss the ball a little bit lower and further from you. Then, when you make contact with the ball you want to hit more outside the ball to generate sidespin. For a kick serve, you want a lot more spin on the ball than the other serves. This makes the ball have a much larger jump after it hits the ground. When you make contact with the ball, you want it to brush up on the ball from the center.

Tips

Learning all of these serves is very benifical as you can switch up what you are doing on your opponent. Just remember that it takes time to build these serves.
