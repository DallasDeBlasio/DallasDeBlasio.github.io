Pacific Rhododendron

Common names

  • Coast Rhododendron
  • California Rhododendron
  • Big Leaf Rhododendron
  • Western Rhododendron

Physical Size

It usually grows to about 4-15 feet. If grown in the shade, however, it can reach 26 feet tall.

Location

this plant can be found in Washington state and extend as south as California and as north as British Columbia. They usually only grow west of the cascades as well.

Climate

Grows best in temperate climate with a lot of moisture. works best with a lot of shade.
